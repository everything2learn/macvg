3D Bowling
Famobi

About

3D Bowling is a fun bowling simlation game where you try to throw the ball as accurate as you can and try to hit as many pins as possible! Use your mouse to throw the ball with different angles and speeds, and whoever knocks down the most pins in the rounds wins the game. You can have a face-off with different bots, or you can play it with another friend or by yourself! Enjoy!

Controls

    Mouse: Aiming / Throwing
